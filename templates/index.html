For the Love of Books

Welcome to For the Love of Books, a site created for those who enjoy reading and want to share their favourite titles with friends and family near or far.

The fast paced world we live in and some long distance friendships make it difficult to find the time to meet at a book club or even find the time to finish the book we picked up some weeks ago. On this site you can add new books titles and authors or add a review at any time. There are no restrictions we only ask that you 'keep it clean' and friendly.

It's time to make yourself a cup of tea, get comfortable and enjoy a few minutes of relaxation with your next favourite book. We'll be looking forward to your additions...
